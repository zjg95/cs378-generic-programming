Parameters:

Returns:
an Integer object whose value is the sum of the original caller and rhs If both signs are positive, the answer will be positive. Example: 14 - (-6) = 14 + 6 = 20 If both signs are negative, the answer will be negative. Example: -14 - (+6) = -14 - 6 = -20 If the signs are different subtract the smaller absolute value from the larger absolute value. The sign will be the sign of the integer that produced the larger absolute value. Example: 14 - (+6) = 14 - 6 = 8 Example: -14 - (-6) = -14 + 6 = -8
